Focus-AI is a one-person studio on a mission to make AI-powered tools accessible, affordable, and genuinely useful for everyday people — starting with India.

Our Mission
Make AI work for the person, not the other way around.
We believe AI tools should feel like a trusted colleague — one that knows your goals, understands your patterns, and proactively helps you improve. Not a chatbot. Not a gimmick. A real productivity partner.
01
AI with purpose
We don't add AI for the sake of it. Every AI feature in our apps solves a real problem users actually face — not a feature checkbox.
02
Simple by default
Power matters, but clarity matters more. We design products that feel approachable on day one and powerful over time.
03
Privacy matters
Your data belongs to you. No ads, no data selling, no dark patterns. Full transparency about what we collect and why.
04
Ship fast, learn fast
We'd rather ship something real and improve it than spend years perfecting it in secret. Feedback shapes everything we build.
