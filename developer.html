How Focus-AI started
THE PROBLEM
Todo apps don't help you achieve goals
I tried every productivity app out there. They all did the same thing — stored my tasks and reminded me. None of them helped me figure out what to do or why I kept failing the same goals.
THE SOLUTION
An AI coach that plans for you
FocAI was born from frustration. I combined Spring Boot, React, and Claude AI to build an app that doesn't just store tasks — it generates personalised plans and coaches you through them.
